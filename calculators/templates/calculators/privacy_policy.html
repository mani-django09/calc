Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Information We Collect

At Calculator.net, we are committed to protecting your privacy. We collect minimal information to provide our services:

Information You Don't Need to Provide

  • No registration or account creation required
  • No personal information needed to use calculators
  • No calculation data is stored on our servers

Automatically Collected Information

  • Basic analytics data (page views, session duration)
  • Technical information (browser type, device type)
  • IP address for security and analytics purposes

How We Use Your Information

We use the limited information we collect to:

  • Improve our website performance and user experience
  • Understand which calculators are most popular
  • Ensure website security and prevent abuse
  • Analyze traffic patterns to optimize our services

Cookies and Local Storage

We use cookies and local storage to enhance your experience:

Essential Cookies

  • Session management and security
  • Remembering your calculator preferences

Analytics Cookies

  • Google Analytics for website usage statistics
  • Performance monitoring and optimization

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect website functionality.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • All calculations are performed in your browser
  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • No sensitive calculation data is transmitted to our servers

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. Google Analytics collects anonymous information about your visit. You can opt-out using Google's opt-out tools.

Content Delivery Networks

We use CDNs to deliver website assets faster. These services may collect basic technical information.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your privacy:

  • Access: Request information about data we collect
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics tracking

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@calculator.net
  • Contact form: Contact Us
